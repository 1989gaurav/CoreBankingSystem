Product Function  

The various sections of the product are described below with their function:

    1. Personal Tab:
      This portion shall be useful only for the employees as it shall ensure the administrative work such as maintainence of different types of account and loan details, transaction functions and few other jobs. Owing to such confidential functions, access to this shall be possible only through a secure method of logging in.

    2. Rural Sector:
      This tab is primarily created so as to cater to the increasing needs of the rural sector in setting up various farming phenomenon. It also has special specification for small-scale industries. Besides, all the Rural Branches are also handled under this section.

    3. International Banking:
      It includes features such as provision of foreign currency loans, financial services for the purpose of import/export, Treasury services, etc.

    4. Corporate Banking:
      Under this section, we provide capital finance to both domestic as well as international users. It also includes many term loans, deferred payment guarantees, etc.

    5. Services:
      Under this tab we handle many exclusive features which are totally for the use of customers. This includes e-Banking, Bill payment, Tax Payments, Letters of Guarantee etc.

    6. Help:
      This is an attached documentation that will act as a user guide to users whether a customer, employee or administrator.
